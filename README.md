Water Tracker App


A simple web app to track daily water intake.
Users click on small cups to represent drinking water, and the app updates the progress towards a 2-liter goal.

Features

Click to fill cups and track water consumption.
Displays progress percentage and remaining water.
Responsive design.

How to Use

Open index.html in a browser.
Click on the small cups to track your water intake.
The progress bar updates as you fill the cups.

Files

index.html: Structure of the app.
style.css: Styling.
script.js: Logic for tracking and updating progress.
